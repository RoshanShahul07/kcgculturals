TRP

  • 5 to 7 members per team.
  • Time limit : 10 minutes.
  • Spoof a television channel of your wish. The act must comprehend to only one TV Channel.
  • Humor, content, and creativity wins the whole show.
  • Recordings or background score can be used.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity will never be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  • Props used do not hold any value.

    • Mounam Pesiyadhe

  • 8 members per team (minimum 5).
  • Time limit : 8- 10 minutes.
  • Tell us something interesting, exciting or make us emotional with your expressions. Speak through your eyes and actions.
  • The team can perform a mime in any concept of their choice.
  • Background music can be used but no recorded voices are allowed.
  • Mask code: white for face, black for the brows and red for lips. Mask code should be strictly followed.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity will never be tolerated and will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.

    • Fully Filmy

  • 4 members per team(3 Minimum).
  • Time limit : 12 - 15 minutes.
  • Event consists of three rounds.
  • First round : Expectation Vs Reality.
  • We face a lot of situations where we expect something but end up with something else. So here you have to enact a play “Expectation Vs. Reality” for a given situation.
  • Second Round: Audio Dub.
  • What if you could dub an Hollywood film in your own funny way?
  • Final Round: Director in You.
  • What if 5 to 10 famous directors worked in the same movie? In this aspect, enact your own movie in the given genre.

    • Ad Mad

  • 5 members per team.
  • Time limit : 8 - 10 minutes.
  • Troll your product, but keep in mind it should be promoted.
  • The product should be imaginary and should be of use among the current society.
  • Creativity, humour and products’ value are the key judging criteria.

    • Street Play

  • Maximum team size : 20 members.
  • Time limit: 15 - 25 minutes.
  • The play may be in either English or Tamil.
  • The play should deliver a social message.
  • Negative points for exceeding the time limit. An introduction of participants is necessary after the play but not included in the time limit.
  • The play must EXACTLY follow your script sent at the time of registration. Any deviations without prior approval will result in disqualification.
  • Points will be deducted for vulgarity.
  • Use of microphones, lights and speakers by any member of the team is not allowed.
  • Makeup and body paint are allowed, however, indicative costumes that are different from the rest of the team are not allowed.
  • No props will be provided. Teams may use props if they want, for which prior approval from the Coordinator of Department of Theatre is necessary. Points will be deducted for the usage of props without permission. (Any item that is not a part of clothing is a prop.)
  • Any liquids or substance that compromises the stage are not allowed.
  • There will be a roll call for participants. No extra members are allowed for common call.
  • Teams must arrive at the venue 45 minutes before the event starts.

    • Mono Act

  • It’s a solo event.
  • Time limit: 5-12 minutes.
  • The play may either be in Tamil/English.
  • It can be a monologue or mimicry.
  • Provisions for microphone will be made.
  • Use of light-weighted props is allowed.
  • Music clip must be arranged by the participant himself.
