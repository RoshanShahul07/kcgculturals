Rules

Two Shades

  • A maximum of 2 members are allowed per team.
  • Time limit: 120 minutes.
  • There can be only two colors in your artwork - black and white.
  • The theme of the event will be declared on spot.
  • Teams can bring their own materials.

    • Music Meets Illustration

  • It’s a solo event.
  • Time limit : 90 minutes.
  • Bring out the song lyrics in a sketch.
  • Song will be given on the spot.
  • Participants should bring their own materials.

    • Splash

  • The maximum team size is 4.
  • Time limit: 120 minutes.
  • This is a face/hand painting event.
  • One member of the team has to volunteer to get his/her face/hand-painted by the rest of the team.
  • The painting must be based on one of the themes announced during the event and must be finished within the stipulated time.
  • At the end of the time duration, the participants must explain their work to the judges.
  • Judgement will be based on creativity, detailing, painting skills and the overall outlook of the artwork.

    • Digital Designing

  • Individual event.
  • Time limit : 60 minutes.
  • Participants are required to bring their laptops along with Adobe Photoshop or Adobe Illustrator.
  • The topic will be given on the spot.
